Questions about partnering.

How quickly can we launch?

In days. There's no platform to build or infrastructure to host — once you're enabled and branded, you can take a working AI SOC to your clients straight away.

Can it be entirely our brand?

Yes. White-label is end to end — portals, domains and reports carry your identity. You can run it co-branded, fully branded, or with Vokter hidden entirely.

Do we need our own analysts?

Not necessarily. With the Autonomous and Guardian modes the first line — and critical-case response — can be fully delivered for you, so you can offer a complete service even without a SOC team.

How do commercial terms work?

They're agreed per partnership and model. Partners earn margin on what they resell or deliver; the specifics are set out when we establish the agreement together.

What support do partners get?

Onboarding, technical enablement, branded sales materials and ongoing support from the Nordic SOC team — so you can sell and deliver with confidence.

Where is client data hosted?

In the EU. Everything runs inside the EU region you and your clients choose — the AI included — so you can sell on genuine sovereignty, not a promise.
