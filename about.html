Why we built Vokter.

Enterprise-grade protection has always demanded enterprise-grade headcount — a 24/7 team, a SIEM to feed, and analysts buried in alerts. We built Vokter to change that equation. Our AI SOC runs the first line of defense at machine speed, contains threats in seconds, and keeps human expertise for the decisions that genuinely need it — all inside the EU, under our own operation.

Outcomes, not tools

We deliver security as a managed result, not another platform for clients to staff and operate.

Sovereignty first

Data is stored, processed and analysed inside the EU — the AI included. Sovereignty is built in, not promised.

Transparent by default

Every action is verified, reversible and explained — so clients always see what was done and why.

People where it counts

The AI carries the volume; our analysts apply judgement to the cases that need experience.
